About Us

Welcome!

Thank you for trusting us to care for your beloved pet. We understand that your furry family members deserve love, attention, and dependable care when you're away. Our mission is to treat each pet as if they were our own — with gentle care, respect, and individualized attention.

Misti's Story

I have loved and owned animals as long as I can remember. I was raised with dogs and other small animals! My daughter and I lost our Ollie in August 2022 at 4 years old to lung cancer, and would love to care for your pet until we're ready for our next family member. I'm comfortable taking care of pets of any age, as well as administering medications (including injectables).

Whether it's daily walks, playtime, fresh meals, or snuggles while you're away — we're here to ensure your pet feels happy and secure. 🐶🐱
